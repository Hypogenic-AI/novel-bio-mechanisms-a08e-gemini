   - Authors: Elana Simon, James Y. Zou
   - ArXiv: 2412.12101

   - Authors: Etowah Adams, et al.
   - ArXiv: 2406.04093
   - Why relevant: Directly addresses using SAEs to generate hypotheses for unknown biological mechanisms.

3. **Evolutionary-scale prediction of atomic-level protein structure with a language model** (2022)
   - Authors: Lin et al. (Meta AI)
   - ArXiv: 2207.03061
   - Why relevant: Foundational paper for ESM-2, the base model for the interpretability studies.
